Uganda

Uganda is a country in East Africa. It borders Kenya in the east, Rwanda in the West, South Sudan in the North, and Congo in the Northeast. Uganda has a population of about 50 million people, and the majority are youths. The people are calm and hospitable as described by many tourists from other parts of the world.Uganda has many mountains and is also home to the source of the Nile River. Rafting is another popular recreational activity.
